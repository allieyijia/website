Hi, I'm Allie.

I'm a UX Engineer and designer graduating from NJIT in May 2026 with a degree in Human-Computer Interaction and a minor in Digital Business and Innovation. I specialize in data-heavy products: dashboards, analytics platforms, and AI-enabled tools that make complex information feel navigable.

What makes me a little different: I can take a design from Figma all the way into production code. My time at UPS taught me that the best UX decisions happen at the intersection of design intent and technical reality — and that you make better decisions when you understand both sides.

I care most about the moment when a confusing system becomes clear. When a dashboard goes from overwhelming to obvious. When a user stops fighting an interface and starts trusting it. That's what I design for.
